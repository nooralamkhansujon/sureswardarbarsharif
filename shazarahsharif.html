Family History

The family of hazrat shah sufi syed fateh ali waisi (R.A) is the progeny of prophet hazrat muhammad sallallaho alaihiwasallam. hazrat fatema (A.S) and hazrat ali (A.S) were the early great parents of the family. An early branch of the family migrated from hejaj to iraq. gradually a branch of the family arrived chittagong of bengal via iran and delhi. they settled in amira bazar village of cittagong (islamabad pargana)

he was a saint and a learned scholar in arabic & persian.

Father: Hazrat syed wares ali (peace be upon him) .

He participated in the jihad (holy war) for establishing islamic state under the command of his pir ( spiritual leader ) amir-ul-mumeenin (commander of the faithful) hazrat syed ahmad shaheed brelavi (R.A.).during june 1829 AD, he was martyred in a battle in the mountainous region of punjab.

Mother: Hazrat syeda saida khatoon (R.A.)

she was a dignified pious lady and hafez-e-quran. soon after the martydom of her husband hazrat syed wares ali (R.A.), she alongwith her youngest son hazrat syed fateh ali boarded a ship in calcutta to migrate to hejaj of arabia. the ship drowned in the sea near calcutta and she departed for the eternal life. hazrat shah sufi syed fateh ali (R.A) survived miraculously.

Life of hazrat syed fateh ali waisi(R.A.):

hazrat shah sufi syed fateh ali waisi (R.A) was born between 1820-23 AD i na village named amira bazar under satkania police station of chittagong district (in bangladesh). Both his father and mother were pious and educated. after the martyrdom his father and death of his mother, he recieved education from haji muhammad mohsin madrasa of hoogly. then he performed oath of allegiance to holy saint hazrat shah sufi noor muhammad nizampuri (R.A) and became his spiritual disciple and subsequently his viceroy. hazrat sufi noor muhammad nizampuri (R.A) was a disciple and viceroy of the commander of the faithful hazrat syed ahmad shaheed brelavi (R.A). for his livelihood he served as employee under several persons and offices, including calcutta high court, nawab shah wajed ali saheb and calcutta political pension office. by the grace of almighty allah he became a highly pious and dignified sufi saint blessed with frequent sipiritual communication with holy prophet hazrat muhammad sallallaho alaihiwasallam. thus communication with hazrat wais qarani (R.A) and therefore was named "waisi ". he had 2 sons and 2 daughters. his second son and second daughter died in early age. he assigned his eldest daughter sufi saint syeda zohora khatoon (R.A) to be in his spiritual position and to become his chief viceroy after his death. he delegated khelafat (viceroyship) to his 35 most obedient disciples including hazrat shah sufi syed ahmed ali @ jan sharif shah sureswari (R.A). His famous work in persian " diwan-i-waisi " was first published from calcutta in 1898. it contains 23 quasida and 179 gazals. 10 of its gazals were included in the higher secondary persian syllabus of calcutta university in 1973. and also included persian syllabus of dhaka university. he passed away to the almighty allah in the year 1886 AD, vide 1293 bengali year and 1308 hijri year on the 8th rabiul awwal, 20th aghrahayan, sunday at 4 pm.
